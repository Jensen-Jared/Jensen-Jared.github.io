It was early summer when you came upon the map. You have always craved adventure and here is your chance to have one. The perils will be real, but the promised fortune is vast if your undertaking is fortuitous. You must assemble a party of adventurers to aid you in your quest. The map says that at least four are required to avoid certain doom. Enter the information for each party member, one at a time:






What's this? There is a message hidden on the map's border. It says the eye color of each member of your group is of the utmost importance. Thank goodness you caught that! To depart without this crucial information would surely bring calamity. First, choose a member of your team using the buttons above, then enter their eye color below and press the button. This will add the ".eyecolor" property.

One final check before we start our quest. The map is adamant that the nomenclature be correct for the travellers chosen. Did you give everyone the right name? This is your last chance to change their name if you like. I assure you that not taking these instructions seriously will bring tragedy upon your little band. Just select which party member using the buttons above and then enter their new name in the space below. Press the button after doing so, and their profile should show their new name.

With all the details properly in order, you should be ready for your adventure. When you have mustered up the courage, press onward!
